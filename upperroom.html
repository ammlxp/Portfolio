UpperRoom Typeface

Dec 23 - Feb 24

This is a typeface inspired by the South London Gallery Fire Station. The building was originally designed to house the firefighters and their families on the top floors, and to store the equipment on the bottom floor. This typeface artfully captures the juxtaposition of the laborious lower level and the tranquil upper floors. Beyond its typographic significance, UpperRoom serves as a personal homage to the building.
