About

I’m a graphic designer based in London with a love for typography and audience-centered design. I thrive in collaborative spaces and aim to create work that is more personal to people. Typically this includes themes of faith, culture, and history.


My practice is shaped by a mix of interests including calligraphy, coding, and I love weaving them together in unexpected ways. Whatever the medium, my goal is always the same: to centre the audience and evoke emotion through thoughtful design.
